Research Profile

Satellite navigation and intelligent positioning under challenging signal conditions.

I develop robust positioning and sensing methods for weak, intermittent, jammed, and time-uncertain satellite-navigation environments. My work combines statistical signal processing, receiver-side algorithms, and artificial intelligence.
