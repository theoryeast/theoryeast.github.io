01 · About

Turning uncertain signals into reliable position.

I am an M.S. student in the School of Electronic Information and Electrical Engineering at Changsha University.

My research centers on satellite navigation and intelligent positioning. I am particularly interested in how receivers can remain useful when signals are weak, intermittent, jammed, or paired with incomplete timing information.

My work spans pseudorange filtering, statistical signal modeling, assisted GNSS, vehicle attitude determination, and learning-based sensing.
